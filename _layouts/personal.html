Job Market

I will be on the 2022/23 job market. My job market paper is joint work with Kai Barron and investigates to what extent people can use narratives to persuade others to hold certain beliefs. We investigate narrative persuasion in an experiment where advisors send narratives about the performance of a hypothetical company to investors. We find that narratives are effective in persuading investors and evaluate interventions that aim to protect investors from falling for false narratives. You can access the paper here.
